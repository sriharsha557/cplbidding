# CPL Player Valuation Framework

## Base Price Setting Strategy

### Performance-Based Tier System

## üèè **BATSMEN VALUATION**

### **Tier 1: Superstar Batsmen (50-70 tokens)**
**Criteria:**
- International experience (50+ matches)
- Consistent high scores (Average 35+ in T20s)
- Match-winning ability
- Leadership qualities
- Brand value

**Examples:** Top international players, proven match-winners
**Base Price Range:** 50-70 tokens

### **Tier 2: Established Batsmen (30-49 tokens)**
**Criteria:**
- Domestic cricket experience (100+ matches)
- Good T20 record (Average 25-35)
- Consistent performer
- Some international exposure

**Examples:** Domestic stars, emerging internationals
**Base Price Range:** 30-49 tokens

### **Tier 3: Promising Batsmen (15-29 tokens)**
**Criteria:**
- Young talent (Under 25)
- Good domestic record
- Limited experience but high potential
- Academy graduates

**Examples:** Youth players, uncapped talents
**Base Price Range:** 15-29 tokens

### **Tier 4: Utility Batsmen (8-14 tokens)**
**Criteria:**
- Squad fillers
- Part-time players
- Limited track record
- Emergency options

**Base Price Range:** 8-14 tokens

## üìä **VALUATION FACTORS & MULTIPLIERS**

### **Performance Metrics (40% weightage)**
- **Batting:** Average, Strike Rate, Consistency
- **Bowling:** Average, Economy Rate, Wickets/Match
- **Fielding:** Catches, Run-outs, Athletic ability

### **Experience Factor (25% weightage)**
- **International:** +20-30% premium
- **Domestic Star:** +10-15% premium
- **Emerging:** Base price
- **Unproven:** -10-20% discount

### **Age Factor (15% weightage)**
- **Peak Age (24-30):** Base price
- **Young (18-23):** +5-10% (potential premium)
- **Veteran (31-35):** -5-10% (experience vs decline)
- **Old (35+):** -15-25% (high risk)

### **Fitness & Availability (10% weightage)**
- **Fully Available:** Base price
- **Injury Concerns:** -10-15% discount
- **Limited Availability:** -20-30% discount

### **Market Demand (10% weightage)**
- **High Demand Role:** +10-15% premium
- **Scarce Skill:** +15-25% premium
- **Oversupply:** -10-15% discount

## üéØ **PRACTICAL BASE PRICE SETTING**

### **Step 1: Player Assessment**
```
Base Score = Performance Score + Experience Bonus + Age Factor + Fitness Factor + Market Factor
```

### **Step 2: Role-Specific Adjustments**
- **Captaincy Potential:** +10-20 tokens

- **Overseas Quota:** +15-25 tokens (if limited slots)

### **Step 3: Market Calibration**
- Compare with similar players
- Adjust for auction dynamics
- Consider team needs

## üîÑ **DYNAMIC PRICING STRATEGIES**

### **Pre-Auction Adjustments**
1. **Market Research:** Check similar player prices in other leagues
2. **Team Needs Analysis:** Identify high-demand positions
3. **Supply-Demand:** Adjust for player availability in each category

### **During Auction Adjustments**
1. **Bidding Patterns:** Monitor early round spending
2. **Budget Constraints:** Teams running low may underbid
3. **Category Completion:** Late category players may get premium

### **Post-Auction Learning**
1. **Price Analysis:** Compare final prices vs base prices
2. **Market Trends:** Identify overvalued/undervalued categories
3. **Future Adjustments:** Refine base price model

## üé≤ **RISK MANAGEMENT**

### **High-Risk Players (Reduce base price by 15-25%)**
- Injury-prone players
- Inconsistent performers
- Attitude issues
- Limited format experience

### **Safe Bets (Premium pricing)**
- Consistent performers
- Good fitness record
- Professional attitude
- Versatile skills

### **Wildcard Pricing**
- Set 10-15% of players at very low base prices
- Creates auction excitement
- Allows for surprise discoveries
- Gives smaller teams chances
